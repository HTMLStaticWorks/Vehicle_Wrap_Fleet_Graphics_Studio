Our Advantage

Why Choose Velocibrand?

Not all wrap studios are created equal. Here's what separates our studio's capabilities from standard print shops and sign companies.

Certified Installers Only

Every wrap technician on our team holds active PDAA or 3M certification. We don't outsource installations — all work is performed in-house in our climate-controlled facility.

Fast 48-Hour Turnaround

Fleet vehicles can't stay off the road. Our streamlined production workflow and dedicated installation bays allow most projects to be completed and returned within 48 working hours.

7-Year Warranty Standard

Every installation leaves our facility backed by a 7-year warranty covering UV fading, delamination, cracking, and edge lifting — included standard with every premium wrap service.
