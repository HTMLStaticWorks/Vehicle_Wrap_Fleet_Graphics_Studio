Consistency First

Corporate Brand Consistency Controls

Maintaining exact corporate color consistency across different vehicle types, base paints, and vinyl sheets can be challenging. Our studio utilizes advanced spectro-photometer color-calibration tools to match Pantone values within strict tolerances.

Whether we are wrapping 5 delivery vans in California or 50 logistics trailers in Michigan, we print using identical profiles and premium vinyl rolls to ensure cohesive brand alignment across your entire transit fleet.

1. Fleet Branding Strategy

Analyzing vehicle paths and regional exposure to propose the optimal balance between full wraps, partial graphics, and safety reflectivity.

2. High-Tech Color Control

Digital color proofs, strict printer calibrations, and standardized printing materials matching your corporate guideline values exactly.

3. Multi-location Rollouts

Synchronized fleet graphics printing and installation scheduling across different logistics depots to minimize transit route downtime.

4. National Fleet Programs

Dedicated accounts manager handling template logistics, repair prints in case of accidents, and warranty claim procedures.
