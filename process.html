How We Deliver

The Way We Work

Our systematic, 8-step project lifecycle ensures absolute wrap precision, material durability, and design consistency.

1

Step 1: Discovery Call

We discuss your project budget range, quantity of assets, vehicle types, layout goals, and schedule timeline details.

2

Step 2: Brand Review

Our vector design group reviews your corporate guidelines, logos, custom typography, and color values (Pantone codes) for correct scaling.

3

Step 3: Vehicle Measurements

We carry out laser-accurate measurements or capture high-res photographs to build custom 3D vehicle template templates for design overlays.

4

Step 4: Creative Design

Our wrap designers arrange vector graphics onto 3D layout proofs, avoiding distortion near vehicle contours, door cuts, and hinges.

5

Step 5: Approval

We submit interactive 3D layout templates or high-fidelity print proofs for corporate procurement teams' review and final approval.

6

Step 6: Production

Graphics are printed on high-end wide-format printers using UV-stable inks on premium cast films and finished with protective laminates.

7

Step 7: Installation

Certified wrap installers clean vehicle body panels and apply vinyl wraps inside a climate-controlled, dust-free installation facility.

8

Step 8: Quality Inspection

We inspect wraps, heat-set vinyl edges to lock memory, and check alignments before delivering vehicles back into service.

9

Step 9: Client Delivery & Handover

Finished vehicles are professionally cleaned, documented with high-resolution photography, and returned to your fleet coordinator with full warranty paperwork and care guides.

10

Step 10: Ongoing Support & Repair

Our dedicated aftercare team handles accident repair reprints, panel replacements, and warranty claims throughout the life of the wrap program.

Premium Materials

Industry-Leading Technology

We source only the finest cast vinyl films, UV-stable laminates, and precision-calibrated wide-format printing systems to guarantee world-class results.

3M Controltac Cast Film

Pressure-activated repositionable adhesive technology eliminates air bubbles during application and maintains full graphic integrity across complex curves and rivets.

UV-Stable Inks & Laminates

High-density UV-cured pigments printed on premium cast substrates and finished with optically clear protective overlaminates resisting yellowing, cracking, and fading for 7+ years.

Wide-Format Printing Systems

Epson SureColor and HP Latex wide-format printers calibrated to a Delta-E <2 color accuracy standard — ensuring Pantone-true corporate color matching across every print run.

7-Year Guarantee

Wrap Warranty & Aftercare Program

Every wrap installation is backed by our comprehensive 7-year warranty covering material failure, UV fading, edge lifting, and delamination — giving your fleet the ultimate protection.

Our post-installation care team conducts 6-month check-in inspections for fleet accounts, providing free edge resealing and graphic alignment adjustments throughout the warranty period.
